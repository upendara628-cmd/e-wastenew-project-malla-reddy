Why E-Waste Management Matters

Electronic waste contains hazardous substances harmful to people and the environment when disposed improperly. Effective e-waste management:

  • Prevents soil and water contamination from toxic chemicals.
  • Conserves natural resources by recovering valuable materials.
  • Reduces greenhouse gas emissions by lowering demand for mining and manufacturing.
  • Generates jobs in recycling and refurbishing sectors.
  • Encourages sustainable technology lifecycle practices.

Every device recycled responsibly helps protect the planet and supports a circular economy for future generations.
