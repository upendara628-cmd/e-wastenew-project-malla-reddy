What if E-Waste is Dumped Carelessly?

Careless disposal releases toxins polluting soil, water, and air. Hazardous materials like lead, mercury, and cadmium affect ecosystems and human health. Responsible recycling protects communities and recovers valuable resources.

How People Are Affected

Communities near informal e-waste processing face respiratory illnesses, developmental issues, and chronic health risks. Preventing toxic exposure is vital for public health and sustainable living.
