What Happens When E-Waste Is Dumped Without Care?

Did you know?

  • 70% of heavy metals in landfills come from e-waste.
  • Only 17.4% of e-waste is properly recycled worldwide.
  • Over 50 million tons are generated each year worldwide.

⚠️ Careless e-waste dumping is more dangerous than you think!

  • ☣️ Toxic danger: Lead, mercury, and cadmium from electronics leach into soil and water, polluting our environment and food supply.
  • 🌬️ Deadly air: Burning e-waste releases poisonous fumes and endangers workers—especially children.
  • 💎 Wasted resources: Every device tossed means lost gold, copper, and rare earths.
“The problem isn’t just pollution—it’s also missing the chance to protect people and nature.”
